salad

Description

Raw greens (such as lettuce) often combined with other vegetables and toppings and served especially with dressing.

Small pieces of food (such as pasta, meat, fruit, or vegetables) usually mixed with a dressing (such as mayonnaise) or set in gelatin.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cucumber
  • Baked Beans
  • Sweet corn and corned beef
  • pasta or potatoes
  • lettuce and cabbage
  • carrots and spring onions

Procedure

  1. Rinse all the vegetables except the eggs and potatoes
  2. Cut potatoes into chunks or as desired and slice hard boiled eggs
  3. Arrange the vegetables as you would like in a clean and dried salad bowl, add sweet corn, corned beef (if using), baked beans and boiled eggs, cover and set aside in the fridge until needed.
  4. When you are ready to use the salad, mix it all together, add your salad dressing and enjoy.
